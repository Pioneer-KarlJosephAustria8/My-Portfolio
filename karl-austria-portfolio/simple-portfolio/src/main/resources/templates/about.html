I never chose BSIT, it wasnt even in my radar when I chose what course I would take. I chose Civil Engineering as my main Course. But after just 1 semester I realized that I am not fit to being a Engineer, the mental fatigue is just too much for me and I decided to just drop it. Over the course of months of me not being in college I contemplated on what should I do. do I just work? no I decided to try again and this time I chose IT. and Im glad I chose well. I ended up being much happier and more proactive on my subjects, and my confidence is better. Im very thankful and I wouldnt have it any other way.
