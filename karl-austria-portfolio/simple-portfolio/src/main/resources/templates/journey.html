My College Journey

Favorite Subject

My favorite subject is anything related to Philosophy. with things like Mathematics or Coding those are just logic, you can question the subject but in the end it results in the same solution/answer. but with Philosophy I get to question a lot of things, like "Why live why do this because we will all die anyway" things like that. I love being curious, it never hurt nobody and all Im doing is satiating my curiosity for new knowledge no harm done.

Memorable Class Experience

Im not one to remember things in school unless its the subjects itself, but I do have fond memories. submitted a research paper and it passed in an international journal, so whoever is reading this if you ever make a research of your own regarding use of AI in college I hope my research would provide you with the knowledge and help I can provide.

Biggest Challenge in College

The biggest challenge for me was adapting to the College Mindset. its not like Highschool anymore where you dont have to study and still get lucky with a minimum grade. In college just the bare-minimum is not enough for me. When I went to College I decided that my days of just cruising along is over and I decided that I will strive to get good grades so that my future would be bright.

What I Have Learned So Far

I have learned a lot since I started college. Not just about programming and technology, but also about time management, working with a team, and not giving up when things get hard. College is not easy but it is teaching me a lot of valuable lessons that I will carry with me for the rest of my life and I hope to never forget these lessons.
